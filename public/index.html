Catharsis Now’ is a webzine that reimagines the act of storytelling. Usernames have empowered the everyday individual to become expressive and unhinged storytellers, sometimes even more than in face to face interactions. To celebrate how technology has aided us in new types of writing, the zine grabs posts from the subreddit ‘r/offMyChest’ and combines it with an AI writing tool to continue these monologues that talk about guilt, anger and fear in order to alleviate the user’s emotions and share everyday experiences through posts only made in the last 24 hours. A living data visualisation of the current cathartic emotions being felt.
